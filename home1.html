Four spa resorts, two spa hotels & more

Welcome to Champneys

At Champneys, wellness is our specialty. A spa pioneer since 1925, our four elegant countryside health spa resorts have helped guests rest and reenergise for generations. More recently, we have ventured into a decadent new era with two luxury spa hotels; where a little bit of what you fancy is not just on offer, it’s actively encouraged

Known for creating robed and relaxed moments of Champneys calm, we combine ancient practices with ultra modern methods. From our city spa sanctuaries, through our iconic resorts to our luxury hotels, every Champneys is home to innovative world-class treatments in sleek treatment rooms and elegant mani-pedi lounges

Best experienced on a spa day, spa break or wellbeing retreat, you'll also love our cosy relaxation areas, showstopping swimming pools, state-of-the-art fitness studios and high-tech thermal experiences, while dining healthily on nutritionist-approved menus.

Spa Resorts & Hotels

Spa resorts for the ultimate robed experience

The original spa influencer, our iconic health spa resorts create robed and relaxed moments of Champneys calm. Home to innovative world class treatments, sleek treatment rooms, cosy relaxation areas, showstopping swimming pools, high-tech thermal experiences, elegant mani-pedi lounges, nutritionist approved menus, detox retreats and much more; our traditional spa resorts will nourish you from the inside out and bring you back to your best.

Spa Resorts

Luxury hotels that go beyond spa

Grand and glamorous, our picturesque hotels are fairy tale wedding venues and perfectly suited to special occasions. Featuring elegant interiors and gourmet dining, why not celebrate in style with traditional afternoon tea, bespoke cocktails and private dining. Ideal for date nights, staycations and everything in-between, you’ll also find a beautiful Champneys spa nestled away in its own quarters for tranquil moments of calm.
